## Introduction

Welcome to Trys.ai, the **AI One-Stop Generation Platform** where you can experience various AI features including image generation, music creation, video production, and intelligent chatbots. At Trys.ai, your privacy is of utmost importance to us, and this privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

## Information Collection and Use

We collect the following types of data to provide you with a better experience while using Trys.ai:

1. **Account Information**
   - **What We Collect**: This includes your name, email address, and any other information you provide when creating an account.
   - **Purpose**: To manage your account and provide customer support.

2. **Usage Details**
   - **What We Collect**: Information about how you use Trys.ai, including your interactions, AI features accessed, generation requests, and usage frequency.
   - **Purpose**: To analyze user engagement and improve our AI services.

3. **Device Information**
   - **What We Collect**: Data regarding the devices you use to access Trys.ai, such as device type, operating system, and browser type.
   - **Purpose**: To optimize our services for different devices and ensure compatibility.

4. **Cookies**
   - **What We Collect**: Small data files placed on your device that help us track user preferences and improve user experience.
   - **Purpose**: To enhance the functionality of our services and personalize your experience.

5. **Payment and Billing Information**
   - **What We Collect**: Information regarding your payment methods, such as credit card number, billing address, and any details required for transaction processing.
   - **Purpose**: To facilitate billing and payment processing for our services.

6. **AI-Generated Content**
   - **What We Collect**: Content you generate using our AI tools (images, music, videos, chat conversations) may be temporarily stored for processing and quality improvement.
   - **Purpose**: To improve our AI models, ensure service quality, and provide you with better generation results.

## Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously. The data we collect is stored securely on our servers, and we implement a variety of security measures including encryption and access controls to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties, except in the following circumstances:

- To comply with legal obligations or respond to lawful requests from public authorities.
- To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users or others.
- To provide services through trusted third-party partners who assist us in operating our platform or conducting our business, subject to confidentiality agreements.
- To third-party AI service providers for the purpose of generating content, subject to strict data protection agreements.
